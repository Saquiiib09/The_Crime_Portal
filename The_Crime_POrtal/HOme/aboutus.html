Welcome to our website "Central Bureau of Investigation".Our team consist of 4 people, we have made this website for helping police officers in their investigations . This site helps you in finding the criminal in an easy way you can view the case history of any suspect who has already been accused of commiting a crime before , you can check records , or if you don't find any previous cases you can always add a new one.
